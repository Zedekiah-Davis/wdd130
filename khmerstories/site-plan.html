Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to spread information about Cambodian folk tales and culture. It will help readers get a better understanding of general culture and two specific stories.

Audience

The target audience will be teenagers and young adults who likely know very little about Cambodia. The website will present the information at a simple and digestible level that fits best with the audience.
